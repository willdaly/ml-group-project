# ML Group Project Presentation Outline

## Slide 1: Business Problem
- Explain the malware or intrusion-detection problem and why it matters.

## Slide 2: Dataset Selection (NSL-KDD)
- Describe the dataset, why it was chosen, and any limitations.

## Slide 3: EDA Highlights
- Show dataset size, data quality findings, and the most important graphs.

## Slide 4: Feature Engineering and Preprocessing
- Explain categorical encoding, scaling, and target construction.

## Slide 5: Models and Metrics
- Compare the baseline models and justify the evaluation metrics.

## Slide 6: Results and Recommendations
- Interpret the results and recommend next steps for the stakeholder.

## Slide 7: Weekly Code Walkthrough Snippets
- List the code snippets each group member should be ready to explain in class.
